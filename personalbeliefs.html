Personal Beliefs Of Geography
Mario Kreuzberg, Birsfelden
2023
Thanks to the openness of Charles and Manuela, who ran Mario Kreuzberg at SALTS until 2024, I was allowed to set off fireworks inside the space before the opening. In addition to the traces of the explosions on the walls and floors, the typical smell of burnt fireworks was also present throughout the entire exhibition period.
